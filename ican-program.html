ICAN Support Program

What is ICAN Support program?

The ICAN Support Program is a short-term assistance initiative designed to support individuals who are navigating temporary funding limitations, transitions within the NDIS, or urgent support needs. ICAN is needs-based, time-limited, and non-contractual, and operates under strict eligibility and governance controls.

While the NDIS provides long-term funding for approved disability supports, some participants may face periods where essential care is at risk due to delays, exhausted funding or changes in circumstances. ICAN operates alongside the NDIS to provide temporary, ethical support during these moments without undermining participant choice, control or NDIS pricing integrity.

The program is designed to offer reassurance, stability and clarity without undermining participant choice, control or NDIS pricing integrity. All ICAN requests are assessed individually and delivered under strict governance controls, with clear entry and exit pathways. Participation in ICAN is discretionary and does not replace funded supports or guarantee ongoing services.

Reduced-Rate Supports

For participants with low or exhausted Core funding who require short-term assistance while planning or evidencing future supports.


  • 7–12% reduction from the NDIS Price Guide
  • Short-term supports only
  • No lock-in service agreements

Interim Supports

Support for individuals awaiting an NDIS access decision or between plans.


  • Privately funded interim assistance
  • Up to 12% reduced private-fee rate
  • Time-limited support

Respite & STA-Related Relief

Designed to relieve carer burnout and prevent placement breakdowns.


  • Free accommodation and meals
  • Support hours billed to Core Supports only

Urgent Change of Circumstances

For participants facing immediate risk and lacking funded support coordination.


  • Free short-term support coordination
  • Evidence collation and escalation support

Interim Accommodation Assistance

Temporary accommodation support while awaiting SIL or SDA funding decisions.


  • Short-term housing or rental assistance
  • Clear exit planning
  • No tenancy rights created
*Important Information

The ICAN Support Program is not a permanent discount program, a guaranteed entitlement, or a replacement for funded NDIS supports. All assistance is time-limited and subject to assessment and availability.

ICAN QUestionnaire

Here are some frequently asked questions

Phendit Disability & Home Care offers the ICAN (Inclusive Care & Access Network) Support Program, a short-term, discretionary assistance program for participants experiencing funding gaps, waiting for plan approvals, or requiring urgent supports. ICAN is time-limited, participant-focused, and fully compliant with NDIS Practice Standards. Supports are approved individually, include exit planning, and do not restrict participant choice or control.

ICAN supports are delivered under clear governance, including eligibility assessment, risk management, management approval, participant consent, start/end documentation, and exit planning. Pricing language aligns with the NDIS Price Guide, and all actions are audit-documented.

Participants discuss needs with staff, who complete an ICAN eligibility form. Approval is obtained from senior management, and all services are time-limited and documented. ICAN is not guaranteed, and participation does not affect NDIS rights.

Each ICAN participant has: eligibility rationale, risk assessment, start/end date, exit plan, and approval signature. All documentation is retained and available for audit.
