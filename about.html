NDIS Information

Understanding Your NDIS Options

We Support:
  • Self-Managed Participants
  • Plan-Managed Participants
  • Agency-Managed (Subject to NDIS approval)
+61404335972

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) supports eligible Australians with funding to access disability supports. It is designed to help you live independently and achieve your personal goals.

Self-Managed: You pay us directly.
Plan-Managed: We invoice your plan manager.
Agency-Managed: Subject to NDIS approval. Not sure? We can help explain your options.

It's simple:
1. Contact us or submit a referral.
2. We discuss your needs and goals.
3. We confirm funding and match you with the right support workers.
4. Services commence.
